What is We Make?

Join the We Make movement. Makers across the UCLA campus and Los Angeles community will join to showcase their inventions, crafts, talents, and innovations. The experience will inspire your inner creative side as you learn more about maker resources on the UCLA campus and explore the innovative work happening in our community.

Exhibitor FAQ

Why We Need You

We Make @ UCLA will not be complete without YOU showcasing your creativity and work to the rest of the UCLA and West Los Angeles Communities. Be a part of UCLA's Festival of Makers! We are now accepting submissions from UCLA Campus Makers and featured Los Angeles Community Makers to showcase their work and to inspire others to think, create, and make.

What You Get

  1. Each exhibitor will get to showcase what they have made to our UCLA and local West Los Angeles Community.
  2. Each exhibitor will receive one parking pass for convenience day of (if needed).
  3. Each exhibitor will receive snacks and a meal day of.

How to Apply

  1. Submit your form to be an event exhibitor.
  2. Please be as thorough as possible in your submission when describing what your exhibit is and its components. We are working within the guidelines of the UCLA Fire Marshal and want to provide them with the necessary info.
  3. A We Make team member will reach out to confirm we have reviewed your submission and to follow-up about any further details.
  4. Complete the submission form on time. Submissions received after that time may be considered space permitting.

What's Next

  1. If you have any updates to provide us after you submit the form, please email them to wemake@orl.ucla.edu.
  2. Exhibitors will be contacted with final event details two weeks before the event, but are invited to plan on being to campus at 12PM for setup the day of the program, May 16th.

Questions? Contact us at wemake@orl.ucla.edu. We look forward to hearing from you!
