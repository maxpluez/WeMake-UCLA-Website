Calling All Makers!

EXHIBITOR SIGN UP NOW OPEN

Join the We Make Movement. Makers of all skill levels and disciplines join us at the UCLA campus to showcase their inventions, crafts, talents, and innovations. The makers will include student makers, UCLA campus makers, and the Los Angeles community of makers. The day is sure to inspire your inner creative as you learn more about Maker Resources on the UCLA campus and explore the innovative work that is happening in our community.
